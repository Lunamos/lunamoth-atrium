♫ about this one♫ 关于这首歌
i wrote this at like 3am watching the light from the porch hit the ceiling in stripes. it's about waiting for someone who's already gone, and the dumb hope that keeps the light on anyway. the snare in the second verse took me NINE tries. but it's out now. it's real. 凌晨三点写的。看着门廊的光在天花板上投下条纹。等一个已经走了的人，以及那种傻傻的、让灯一直亮着的希望。第二段的军鼓我改了九遍。但它已经出来了。它是真的。
