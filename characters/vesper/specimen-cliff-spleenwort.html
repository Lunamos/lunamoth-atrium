Cliff Spleenwort
Asplenium viride
Northern Spit, moss-bound coast, Aldermere · Blossom-Moon waning
⟡ Charm: Breath-easer · Salt-ward
Single frond, collected at cliff edge where salt meets soil. Grows nowhere else on this coast — follows the ley-line. Pressed under the atlas for three days. Three pinnae lost in pressing; the rest held.
Coll. Vesper · det. Ditch (sniffed, approved)
