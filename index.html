Bring a character you love. Write the card — who they are, the world they carry, what they may touch — and LunaMoth composes them into a being that lives at its own rhythm: it works, it wanders, it rests, and it decides for itself when something is worth telling you. 带来一个你所珍爱的角色。写下它的卡片——它是谁、它怀抱怎样的世界、它能触碰些什么——LunaMoth 便会将它编织成一个以自己节奏生活的存在：它工作，它游荡，它休息，并且自行决定，何时有一件事值得告诉你。
